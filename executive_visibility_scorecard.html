Executive Visibility Scorecard

Answer 16 questions to assess how visible you are to decision-makers — and discover exactly what's blocking your next promotion.

Section 1: LinkedIn Clarity
0–4 points per question
1. Your headline clearly communicates leadership value (not job title only).
0 = job title only  ·  4 = clearly signals leadership scope + outcomes
2. Your About section explains what problems you solve at a leadership level.
0 = missing or generic  ·  4 = clearly positioned for senior roles
3. Your experience section shows measurable business impact (not duties).
0 = task-based  ·  4 = outcome-based with metrics
4. A recruiter or executive can understand your seniority in under 10 seconds.
0 = unclear  ·  4 = instantly obvious
Section 2: Leadership Signal Strength
0–4 points per question
5. Your profile reflects strategic leadership, not execution-only work.
0 = execution-heavy  ·  4 = strong strategic framing
6. You have visible proof of decision-making influence.
0 = none  ·  4 = clear examples of decisions, ownership, or leadership impact
7. Your career story clearly shows progression toward leadership roles.
0 = fragmented  ·  4 = coherent upward narrative
8. You sound like someone who leads, not someone who "supports."
0 = support role framing  ·  4 = leadership identity established
Section 3: Visibility & Discovery
0–4 points per question
9. You show up in recruiter searches for roles one level above your current position.
0 = no visibility  ·  4 = strong discoverability
10. You are regularly contacted for opportunities aligned to your target level.
0 = rarely  ·  4 = frequently
11. Your LinkedIn activity reinforces authority (posts, commentary, insights).
0 = inactive  ·  4 = consistent authority signals
12. Your network includes decision-makers, not just peers.
0 = peers only  ·  4 = strong executive-level network
Section 4: Promotion Readiness Signals
0–4 points per question
13. Your resume/LinkedIn clearly supports a move into management.
0 = IC framing  ·  4 = management-ready positioning
14. You can articulate your leadership value in one sentence.
0 = unclear  ·  4 = strong, repeatable narrative
15. You have documented business impact tied to leadership outcomes.
0 = none  ·  4 = strong quantified impact
16. You are being actively considered for roles above your current level.
0 = no  ·  4 = yes, consistently
